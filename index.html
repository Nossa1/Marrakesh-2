Luxury Riads of Marrakech

Riads are lavish homes with garden courtyards that
have been associated with luxury and hospitality.
They were originally owned by governors and
other famous families. Many of these magnificent buildings
in Marrakech have undergone tremendous renovations
to become private luxury hotels with exquisite features.
